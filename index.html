Our Purpose

Abu Huraira reported: The Messenger ﷺ said:

وَتُمِيطُ الأَذَى عَنِ الطَّرِيقِ صَدَقَةٌ

“Removing harmful objects from the pathway is an act of charity”

(Bukhari & Muslim)

PureStreets is a local volunteer movement inspired by the Prophetic Sunnah. Founded by a group of students, we aim to clean the streets around us with sincerity, good manners, and practical care for the people who walk by them every day.

Everyone has a place here: families, students, mosques, brothers and sisters groups, and neighbours who simply want their area to feel loved.

Aims and Objectives

01

Put Islamic values of stewardship, care for the earth, and cleanliness into action

02

Build positive relationships with our neighbours and the wider community

03

Promote physical and mental wellbeing through active volunteering

04

Strengthen unity within the community and build meaningful connections through shared service
